# About Nguyen Phuong Anh

Phuong Anh is currently pursuing an Executive MBA (VEMBA) at the Shidler College of Business at the University of Hawaii, and holds a Bachelor of Business Administration from Foreign Trade University.

She serves as a Product Management Manager of Loyalty and Engagement Solutions at Vingroup, having previously acted as the Head of Loyalty Domain at One Mount Consumer, where she drove the product roadmap and strategy for a comprehensive loyalty ecosystem.

Phuong Anh's professional experience includes roles as a Product Owner Lead and Product Owner at One Mount Group, where she built a new Core Loyalty System from scratch and conceptualized core features for multiple coalition programs, successfully scaling the platform to serve over 5 million monthly active users.

Before her focus on loyalty ecosystems, she was a Business Development Manager at DesignBold specializing in the South Korean market, and managed startup incubation programs at VNP Group and HATCH! VENTURES.
